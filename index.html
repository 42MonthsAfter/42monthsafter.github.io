42 Months After

3 DayZ Servers. Three styles of game play. 3 times the adventure.

Whether you're into PvE & role playing, PvP deathmatch shananigans, or hard core survival, we have something for you.

On Your Own

A hard core survival, vanilla+ DayZ experience.

This server is for the DayZ purist who is looking for something a little new.

With a few mods to help elevate the challenge and game play style, On Your Own is the ideal place for someone looking for a new challenge without wanting to deviate too far from what makes DayZ DayZ.

Custome zombies, freezing temps, mod cars and helis, traders with limited stock... There is a lot to love about this flavor of 42 Months After.

PvE + Role Play

Looking for the safety to explore Deer Isle with the challange of surviving rare animals and custom zombies? Start here.

You'll spawn in at the Holler, a safe zone + trader, where you can meet up with your friends and set out to explore all that Deer Isle has to offer.

Rare animals for hunting, over 40 kind of fish, Base Building+ with no raiding and a couple PvP zones sprinkled in for extra challenge, this is a great server for those who are new to DayZ or PC and looking to cut their teeth.

Murder Isle

Fancy some run and gun action in DayZ? Check out this newest flavor of 42 Months After.

No zombies. No building. Just you, your squad and higher tier weapons and gear all spawning around the tiny Swan Island, a 100% custom PvP DayZ map.

Bring your friends or run solo to King of the Hills to find specialty weapons and loadouts. Check the leaderboard to see how you are ranked amount other players. Remember though, it resets if you get killed to stalk with the quietness.
